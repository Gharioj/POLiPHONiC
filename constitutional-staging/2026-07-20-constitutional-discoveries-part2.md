## Constitutional Flourishing

*Constitutional Discovery*

### Observation

The purpose of the Constitution is not merely to regulate constitutional participation.

Its purpose is to create and protect the constitutional space within which constitutional participants, constitutional communities and conscious creative expression may flourish.

### Principle

Constitutional flourishing is the continual growth and realisation of constitutional purpose through faithful constitutional participation, contribution, custodianship and stewardship.

### Principle

Every constitutional role should contribute to the flourishing of the constitutional community and the enduring constitutional purpose.

---

## Constitutional Service

*Constitutional Discovery*

### Observation

Every constitutional role exists in service of the constitutional purpose.

No constitutional role exists for its own benefit.

### Principle

Constitutional authority, governance, custodianship and stewardship are all exercised in constitutional service.

Their purpose is to advance, preserve, enhance and enable the flourishing of the constitutional community in accordance with the Constitution.

### Constitutional Test

Every exercise of constitutional responsibility should be capable of answering one constitutional question:

"Whom or what does this faithfully serve?"

If the answer is not the constitutional purpose, the enduring constitutional values and principles, or the flourishing of the constitutional community, it is not a faithful exercise of constitutional responsibility.

## Constitutional Operation

*Constitutional Discovery*

### Observation

The Constitution is not intended merely to exist.

It is intended to live through the faithful constitutional participation, contribution, custodianship, stewardship, governance and service of the constitutional community.

### Principle

The Constitution holds the constitutional space.

The constitutional community brings that space to life through its constitutional conduct.

### Principle

The Constitution demonstrates its value through its operation.

As the Constitution lives, constitutional participants experience its benefits through constitutional participation, contribution, custodianship, stewardship, governance and service.

### Constitutional Progression

Constitutional discovery leads to constitutional understanding.

Constitutional understanding leads to constitutional responsibility.

Constitutional responsibility leads to constitutional stewardship.

Constitutional stewardship enables constitutional governance.

Constitutional governance protects constitutional space.

Constitutional space enables constitutional flourishing.

Constitutional flourishing fulfils the constitutional purpose.

### Observation

The next stage of constitutional development is no longer to ask:

"What is the Constitution?"

The next stage is to ask:

"How does the Constitution live?"

The Constitution is realised through its continual constitutional operation.

## Constitutional Development Observation

This work represents the completion of the current constitutional discovery phase.

The next constitutional phase is no longer primarily concerned with defining constitutional concepts.

The next phase concerns constitutional operation.

Future constitutional development should therefore focus on how the Constitution behaves in practice through constitutional scenarios, including (but not limited to):

- admission of constitutional participants;
- recognition of constitutional custodians;
- exercise of constitutional stewardship;
- constitutional governance;
- constitutional decision-making;
- constitutional review;
- constitutional disagreement and resolution;
- constitutional authority;
- constitutional succession;
- constitutional amendment;
- protection of constitutional integrity;
- preservation of constitutional purpose;
- enabling constitutional flourishing.

This observation is intended to assist repository organisation and future constitutional development. It is not itself constitutional doctrine unless incorporated elsewhere.

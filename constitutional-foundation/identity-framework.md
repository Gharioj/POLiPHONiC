# POLiPHONiC — Constitutional Identity Framework

*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Establishing Constitutional Identity Throughout the Creative Ecosystem.*

## Constitutional Provenance

This publication forms part of the POLiPHONiC Constitutional Library.

It is derived from the Executive Briefing, the Technical Architecture, the Governance Charter, the Founding Partners Charter, the Creator Constitution, the Technical Implementation Specification, the Legal & Constitutional Framework, the Economic Participation Framework, the Constitutional Standards Framework, the Constitutional Operations Framework, the Constitutional Assurance Framework, the Constitutional Interoperability Framework, the Constitutional Innovation Framework and the Constitutional Trust Framework.

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which identity is recognised, protected and preserved throughout the Constitutional Infrastructure.

Accordingly, identity is not merely a technical credential. Identity is the constitutional origin of every recognised creative relationship. Every subsequent constitutional relationship derives from recognised identity.

## Executive Overview

Creativity begins with participation. Participation requires recognition. Recognition begins with identity. Identity therefore establishes the constitutional foundation from which authorship, collaboration, rights, economic participation and legacy are derived.

The Constitutional Identity Framework establishes the enduring principles through which identity remains trusted, secure and continuously connected throughout the complete lifecycle of creative work. Identity is therefore more than authentication. It is constitutional recognition.

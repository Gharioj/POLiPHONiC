Creativity begins with participation. Recognition establishes authorship. Authorship provides the constitutional bridge between creativity and every subsequent constitutional relationship. Through authorship, recognised creative contribution becomes transparently connected to collaboration, rights, economic participation and legacy.

The Constitutional Authorship Framework establishes the enduring principles through which authorship remains authentic, verifiable and continuously preserved throughout the evolution of creative work. Authorship therefore preserves creative contribution through constitutional continuity.

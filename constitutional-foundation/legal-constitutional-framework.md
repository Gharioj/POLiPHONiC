# POLiPHONiC — Legal & Constitutional Framework

*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Establishing the Legal Framework through which Constitutional Infrastructure Operates.*

## Constitutional Provenance

This publication forms part of the POLiPHONiC Constitutional Library.

It is derived from the Executive Briefing, the Technical Architecture, the Governance Charter, the Founding Partners Charter, the Creator Constitution and the Technical Implementation Specification.

Its purpose is to establish the legal framework through which the Constitutional Infrastructure is expressed within applicable legal systems.

Accordingly, this publication does not replace the Constitution. It provides the legal expression through which constitutional principles are recognised, protected and implemented across jurisdictions. Law provides legal effect. The Constitution provides constitutional purpose.

## Executive Overview

The Constitution establishes purpose. Architecture establishes structure. Implementation establishes operation. The Legal & Constitutional Framework establishes legal expression.

Constitutional principles are intended to endure irrespective of jurisdiction. Legal implementation enables those principles to operate within the legal systems through which creators collaborate, establish rights and participate economically. The legal framework therefore exists to preserve constitutional integrity whilst enabling lawful implementation across evolving legal environments.

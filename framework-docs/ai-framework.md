*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Ensuring Artificial Intelligence Remains Derived from Constitutional Purpose.*

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which Artificial Intelligence is designed, governed and applied throughout the Constitutional Infrastructure.

Accordingly, Artificial Intelligence does not determine constitutional purpose. Artificial Intelligence implements constitutional purpose. Every AI capability therefore remains accountable to the constitutional principles from which POLiPHONiC is derived.

## Executive Overview

Artificial Intelligence represents one of the most significant technological developments of the modern creative economy. Its potential is considerable. Its responsibility is equally significant. The Constitutional Infrastructure has therefore been designed to ensure that Artificial Intelligence serves creators rather than replaces them.

AI should strengthen recognition. It should strengthen authorship. It should strengthen collaboration. It should strengthen rights. It should strengthen economic participation. It should strengthen legacy.

The Constitutional AI Framework establishes the enduring principles through which Artificial Intelligence remains transparent, accountable and continuously aligned with constitutional purpose.

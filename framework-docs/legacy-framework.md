*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Preserving Creative Contribution Across Generations.*

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which creative contribution, authorship, rights and cultural value are preserved beyond the lifetime of individual works, technologies and generations.

## Executive Overview

Creativity creates more than individual works. It creates culture. It creates influence. It creates opportunity for those who follow. The Constitutional Infrastructure exists not only to recognise creativity in the present, but to preserve its constitutional continuity for the future.

The Constitutional Legacy Framework establishes the enduring principles through which recognised participation, authorship, collaboration, rights and economic participation remain permanently connected across generations. Legacy therefore represents the constitutional continuity of creativity through time.

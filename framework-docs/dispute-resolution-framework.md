*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Resolving Constitutional Matters Through Integrity, Transparency and Continuity.*

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which disagreements concerning recognised participation, authorship, collaboration, rights, governance and constitutional relationships are resolved.

## Executive Overview

Creative relationships are intended to strengthen collaboration. Occasionally they require resolution. The Constitutional Infrastructure therefore provides a constitutional process through which disagreements may be addressed transparently, fairly and consistently.

Resolution exists to preserve recognised participation, constitutional integrity and enduring constitutional relationships. The Constitutional Dispute Resolution Framework establishes the principles through which constitutional disagreements are resolved whilst preserving confidence in the Constitutional Infrastructure. Resolution therefore restores constitutional continuity rather than creating constitutional authority.

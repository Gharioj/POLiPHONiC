# POLiPHONiC — Economic Participation Framework

*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Establishing the Constitutional Framework for Creative Value and Economic Participation.*

## Constitutional Provenance

This publication forms part of the POLiPHONiC Constitutional Library.

It is derived from the Executive Briefing, the Technical Architecture, the Governance Charter, the Founding Partners Charter, the Creator Constitution, the Technical Implementation Specification and the Legal & Constitutional Framework.

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which recognised creative participation becomes transparent economic participation.

Accordingly, economic participation is not treated as an isolated financial process. It is the constitutional consequence of recognised participation, authorship, collaboration and rights. The Constitution therefore establishes the principles from which all economic participation is derived.

## Executive Overview

Creative value originates with creativity. Recognition establishes authorship. Authorship enables collaboration. Collaboration defines rights. Rights enable economic participation.

The Economic Participation Framework establishes the constitutional principles through which creative value is recognised, allocated, distributed and preserved throughout the complete lifecycle of creative work. Its purpose is not simply financial administration. Its purpose is constitutional confidence.

By preserving transparent constitutional relationships between creativity and economic participation, POLiPHONiC establishes an economic framework intended to serve creators, collaborators and future generations with clarity, accountability and trust.

## 1. Constitutional Purpose of Economic Participation

**Constitutional Principle**
Creative contribution gives rise to economic participation. Economic participation derives from recognised creative contribution. It is not created independently through technology, administration or commercial preference. Economic participation therefore remains continuously connected to the constitutional relationships from which it is derived.

**Constitutional Objective**
To establish a constitutional framework through which recognised creative contribution participates transparently, fairly and confidently in the value generated by creative work.

**Constitutional Design**
- Derive economic participation from recognised creative contribution.
- Preserve constitutional continuity between creativity and value.
- Promote transparency, accountability and confidence.
- Protect the long-term integrity of constitutional economic participation.

**Constitutional Outcome**
Economic participation remains derived from creativity. The Constitution anchors economic participation to purpose.

## 2. Constitutional Economic Principles

**Constitutional Principle**
Economic value follows constitutional relationships. Creative value is generated through recognised participation, enduring authorship and transparent collaboration. Economic participation therefore follows the constitutional relationships from which creative value arises. Value follows creativity.

**Constitutional Objective**
To preserve transparent constitutional relationships between recognised creative contribution and economic value throughout the complete creative lifecycle.

**Constitutional Design**
- Preserve constitutional continuity between creativity and value.
- Maintain transparent constitutional provenance.

Economic value remains derived from constitutional relationships. Confidence strengthens Constitutional Infrastructure.

**Constitutional Principle**
Allocation reflects recognised contribution. Economic participation should reflect recognised creative contribution rather than administrative complexity or institutional preference. Transparent constitutional allocation strengthens confidence between creators, collaborators and participants throughout the creative ecosystem.

**Constitutional Objective**
To establish constitutional principles through which creative value is allocated transparently, consistently and fairly.

**Constitutional Design**

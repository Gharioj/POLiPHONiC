# POLiPHONiC — Constitutional Interoperability Framework

*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Enabling Constitutional Continuity Across Creative, Legal and Technical Ecosystems.*

## Constitutional Provenance

This publication forms part of the POLiPHONiC Constitutional Library.

It is derived from the Executive Briefing, the Technical Architecture, the Governance Charter, the Founding Partners Charter, the Creator Constitution, the Technical Implementation Specification, the Legal & Constitutional Framework, the Economic Participation Framework, the Constitutional Standards Framework, the Constitutional Operations Framework and the Constitutional Assurance Framework.

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which the Constitutional Infrastructure interoperates with external creative, legal, commercial and technical ecosystems.

Accordingly, interoperability does not alter constitutional purpose. It extends constitutional purpose. Every interoperable relationship therefore remains derived from the Constitution.

## Executive Overview

Creativity increasingly develops across interconnected ecosystems. Creators collaborate across organisations, jurisdictions, technologies and industries. The Constitutional Infrastructure must therefore interoperate without compromising constitutional integrity. Interoperability exists to extend constitutional continuity rather than replace it. The Constitution remains the common foundation through which every interoperable relationship is established.

# POLiPHONiC — Constitutional Creative Compacts Framework

*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Establishing the Constitutional Framework for Creative Relationships.*

## Constitutional Provenance

This publication forms part of the POLiPHONiC Constitutional Library.

It is derived from the Executive Briefing, the Technical Architecture, the Governance Charter, the Founding Partners Charter, the Creator Constitution, the Technical Implementation Specification, the Legal & Constitutional Framework, the Economic Participation Framework, the Constitutional Standards Framework, the Constitutional Operations Framework, the Constitutional Assurance Framework, the Constitutional Interoperability Framework, the Constitutional Innovation Framework, the Constitutional Trust Framework, the Constitutional Identity Framework, the Constitutional Data Framework, the Constitutional Provenance Framework, the Constitutional Collaboration Framework, the Constitutional Rights Framework, the Constitutional Authorship Framework, the Constitutional Recognition Framework and the Constitutional Legacy Framework.

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which Creative Compacts are created, governed and preserved throughout the complete lifecycle of creative collaboration.

Accordingly, a Creative Compact is not merely an agreement. It is the constitutional record through which recognised participants establish their creative relationship from the moment participation begins. Every subsequent constitutional relationship derives from the Creative Compact through which collaboration is first recognised.

## Executive Overview

Creative collaboration begins with people. Constitutional collaboration begins with a Creative Compact. The Creative Compact establishes the constitutional relationship between recognised participants before complexity, commercial arrangements or legal agreements emerge.

It preserves recognised participation, authorship, collaboration, rights, economic participation and constitutional provenance throughout the complete lifecycle of creative work. The Constitutional Creative Compacts Framework establishes the enduring principles through which Creative Compacts remain transparent, trusted and continuously connected to constitutional purpose. Creative Compacts therefore become the constitutional foundation of collaborative creativity.

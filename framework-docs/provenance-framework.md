*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Establishing the Constitutional Provenance of Every Creative Relationship.*

This publication forms part of the POLiPHONiC Constitutional Library.

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which provenance is recognised, preserved and continuously maintained throughout the complete lifecycle of creative work.

Accordingly, provenance is not merely historical information. Provenance is the constitutional continuity through which recognised participation, authorship, collaboration, rights, economic participation and legacy remain permanently connected. Every constitutional relationship therefore possesses constitutional provenance.

## Executive Overview

Constitutional continuity depends upon constitutional provenance. Without provenance there can be no trusted recognition. Without trusted recognition there can be no enduring authorship. Without enduring authorship there can be no transparent collaboration, rights or economic participation.

The Constitutional Provenance Framework establishes the principles through which every constitutional relationship remains authentic, verifiable and continuously connected to its constitutional origin. Provenance therefore preserves constitutional memory across generations.

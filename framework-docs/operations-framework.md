# POLiPHONiC — Constitutional Operations Framework

*Constitutional Infrastructure for Creativity — Operating Constitutional Infrastructure with Integrity, Transparency and Continuity.*

## Constitutional Provenance

This publication forms part of the POLiPHONiC Constitutional Library.

It is derived from the Executive Briefing, the Technical Architecture, the Governance Charter, the Founding Partners Charter, the Creator Constitution, the Technical Implementation Specification, the Legal & Constitutional Framework, the Economic Participation Framework and the Constitutional Standards Framework.

Its purpose is to establish the constitutional framework through which the Constitutional Infrastructure is operated on a continuing basis.

Accordingly, operations do not determine constitutional purpose. Operations implement constitutional purpose. Every operational activity therefore remains derived from the Constitution through governance, architecture, implementation and constitutional standards.

The Constitution establishes purpose. Governance preserves purpose. Architecture provides structure. Implementation enables operation. Operations sustain the Constitutional Infrastructure.

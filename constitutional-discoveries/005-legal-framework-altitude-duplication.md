# Constitutional Discovery 005

## Discovery

The Legal & Constitutional Framework's §4 ("Constitutional Rights Framework") and §7 ("Constitutional Dispute Resolution") cover the same subject matter as the dedicated Constitutional Rights Framework and the dedicated Constitutional Dispute Resolution Framework, at a shorter, legal-specific altitude. No contradiction was found between the overlapping sections — but whether the corpus intends this as deliberate layering (a legal summary alongside a full framework) or as duplication that should eventually be reconciled has not been decided.

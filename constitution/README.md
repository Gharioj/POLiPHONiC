# The Constitution

The authoritative, operative text of the POLiPHONiC Constitution — currently **Working Draft 1**.

This directory is distinct from [`constitutional-foundation/`](../constitutional-foundation/README.md), per the architecture given with this integration: the Foundation is the constitutional research and discovery record — 39 documents, preserved unmodified — while this directory holds the constitution actually drawn from that record and now treated as operative. Nothing in the Foundation is replaced, deleted, or overwritten by anything here; both are preserved in full, permanently.

## How Working Draft 1 arrived here

Working Draft 1 was received as eight sequential manuscript sections via chat on 2026-07-20, each appended verbatim to a reception scaffold prepared in advance at [`constitutional-staging/constitutional-drafts/`](../constitutional-staging/constitutional-drafts/README.md). Once the full manuscript was received and its structural integrity verified, it was moved here and marked integrated. See [`working-draft-1.provenance.md`](working-draft-1.provenance.md) for the full receipt history and integrity report — including several numbering gaps, an inconsistent structural labelling scheme, and two pairs of duplicate article titles present in the manuscript as received, none of which has been corrected or reconciled.

## Future drafts

A Working Draft 2, if and when it exists, follows the same path: staged and assembled in [`constitutional-staging/constitutional-drafts/`](../constitutional-staging/constitutional-drafts/README.md), reviewed, and — only once ready — integrated here with a provenance record stating `supersedes: Working Draft 1`. Superseded drafts are not deleted; each remains in this directory's index as part of the permanent constitutional record, mirroring how `constitutional-foundation/` never removes a document once integrated.

## Relationship to other structures

- [`constitutional-foundation/`](../constitutional-foundation/README.md) — the constitutional research and discovery record this draft draws on. Preserved in full; not superseded, replaced, or altered by anything in this directory.
- [`constitutional-staging/constitutional-drafts/`](../constitutional-staging/constitutional-drafts/README.md) — where a complete constitutional draft is received and assembled before integration. Working Draft 1 passed through there; its scaffold now points here.
- [`constitutional-discoveries/`](../constitutional-discoveries/README.md) — open constitutional questions. Working Draft 1's provenance record notes where it speaks to two of them (001, rights vs. ownership; 003, undefined governance authority and process) without resolving either. No discovery is marked resolved by this integration.
- [`constitutional-resolutions/`](../constitutional-resolutions/README.md) — formally recorded rulings. This integration is not itself a resolution of anything, and creates none.
